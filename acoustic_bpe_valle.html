Text-to-Speech under Language Modeling Paradigm with Acoustic BPE

Feiyu Shen1,

1 X-LANCE Lab, Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China

Abstract

In short, this is Text-to-Speech under the language modeling paradigm, similar to VALL-E but with the Encodec token replaced by HuBERT discrete tokens. Also, we encode discete tokens with Acousitc BPE to facilitate faster inference speed and more accurate synthesis.
